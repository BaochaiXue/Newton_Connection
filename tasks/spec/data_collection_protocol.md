# Spec: data_collection_protocol

## Goal

Define a short, repeatable capture protocol that supports reconstruction and
downstream bridge use.

## Non-Goals

- Rewriting the reconstruction stack itself
- Expanding the protocol into a long lab manual no one will follow

## Inputs

- `docs/bridge/tasks/data_collection_protocol.md`
- current capture habits and downstream consumer needs

## Outputs

- concise protocol steps
- explicit save list
- student-handoff-friendly instructions

## Constraints

- keep it short enough to follow in practice
- align with bridge/export and reconstruction needs

## Done When

- the protocol can be followed without oral explanation
- required saves and sequence structure are explicit
