# Spec: video_presentation_quality

## Goal

Keep demo videos readable as evidence rather than decorative media.

## Non-Goals

- Polishing weak demos that should instead be deleted
- Treating automatic PASS as sufficient evidence by itself

## Inputs

- `docs/bridge/tasks/video_presentation_quality.md`
- bridge demo renderers and GIF builders
- validator outputs and skeptical video review artifacts

## Outputs

- better camera/reference defaults
- task-local decisions on which videos remain promotable
- explicit quality gates for readability

## Constraints

- focus on evidence readability
- compare A/B videos under aligned framing

## Done When

- contact/support/process visibility are clear
- comparison videos are visually fair
- weak videos are either fixed or explicitly retired
